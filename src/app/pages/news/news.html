Memoirs of a Side-Piece

Contemporary Fiction • 2026 • 412 Pages

Ahnjzanee Micheel Dubois has never asked permission to live life on her own terms. Beautiful, wealthy, highly educated, and co-owner of a thriving consulting firm, she’s built her success on discipline, independence, and the unwavering belief that needing no one is the ultimate freedom. Love has never been part of the equation.

Everything changes when she lands the biggest deal of her career and meets Bryson Welborne — a brilliant, charismatic corporate executive who is also a devoted husband and father. Their business negotiations evolve into a private arrangement: discreet, passionate, mutually beneficial, and free of emotional obligation.

On paper, it’s the perfect agreement. In reality, desire has a way of rewriting contracts — and some terms are never negotiated.

Memoirs of a Side-Piece is a raw, unflinching novel about desire, self-worth, and the cost of loving in the shadows — told in Johnnie C. Johnson Jr.’s signature voice: honest, emotionally charged, and unafraid to sit in the complicated space between right and real.

What to Expect

  • A raw, first-person voice that doesn’t flinch from hard truths
  • A slow-burn exploration of love, loyalty, and self-worth
  • Richly drawn characters facing impossible choices
  • The emotional depth and page-turning pace readers know him for

The Other Side of Me

Psychological Thriller • 2025

After pioneering a revolutionary treatment for psychiatric disorders, Dr. Sigmund F. Rasniac secretly expands his research to unauthorized experiments on pre-adolescent subjects. Convinced his groundbreaking technique is both flawless and transformative, he pushes the boundaries even further by selecting an unwilling young adult patient who appears to have no past — and nothing to lose.

But some minds are never meant to be altered. Francoise J’Etienne should have been left alone.
