The Other Side of Me -

After pioneering a revolutionary treatment for psychiatric disorders, Dr. Sigmund F. Rasniac secretly expands his research to unauthorized experiments on pre-adolescent subjects. Convinced his groundbreaking technique is both flawless and transformative, he pushes the boundaries even further by selecting an unwilling young adult patient who appears to have no past—and nothing to lose. But some minds are never meant to be altered. Francoise
J' Etienne should have been left alone.
